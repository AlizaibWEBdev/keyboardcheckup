About KeyboardCheckup

Learn about our mission to provide free, reliable keyboard testing tools for users worldwide

Our Story

KeyboardCheckup was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to provide everyone with easy access to reliable keyboard testing tools. We recognized that keyboard issues can be frustrating and time-consuming to diagnose, especially when you're not sure if the problem is with your hardware or software.

Our team of developers and keyboard enthusiasts set out to create a comprehensive, user-friendly solution that works across all devices and operating systems. We believe that testing your keyboard should be as simple as pressing keys and seeing immediate visual feedback.

Today, KeyboardCheckup serves thousands of users worldwide, helping them diagnose keyboard issues, test new keyboards, and ensure their typing tools are in perfect working condition.

Our Mission

At KeyboardCheckup, we're committed to:

  • Providing free, accessible keyboard testing tools for everyone
  • Ensuring our tools work seamlessly across all devices and platforms
  • Continuously improving our testing capabilities based on user feedback
  • Protecting user privacy by running all tests locally in the browser
  • Educating users about keyboard maintenance and troubleshooting
  • Supporting all types of keyboards - mechanical, membrane, wireless, and gaming

What We Offer

KeyboardCheckup provides a comprehensive suite of keyboard testing tools:

Keyboard Tester

Our flagship tool allows you to test every key on your keyboard with real-time visual feedback. See which keys are working, identify ghosting issues, and ensure your keyboard is functioning perfectly.

Typing Test

Measure your typing speed and accuracy with our typing test tool. Track your progress over time and improve your typing skills with regular practice.

Educational Resources

We provide helpful guides and articles about keyboard maintenance, troubleshooting common issues, and choosing the right keyboard for your needs.
