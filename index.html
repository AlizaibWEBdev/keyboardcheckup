Frequently Asked Questions

How does the online keyboard tester work? +
Our keyboard tester displays a virtual representation of a standard keyboard. When you press keys on your physical keyboard, the corresponding keys on the virtual keyboard light up, allowing you to verify that each key is working properly.
Can I test all types of keyboards? +
Yes! Our keyboard tester works with mechanical keyboards, laptop keyboards, wireless keyboards, gaming keyboards, and any other standard USB or Bluetooth keyboard. It's compatible with Windows, Mac, Linux, and Chromebook systems.
What is keyboard ghosting and how can I test for it? +
Keyboard ghosting occurs when a keyboard fails to register multiple simultaneous key presses. To test for ghosting, press several keys at once and check if all are highlighted on our virtual keyboard. Gaming keyboards typically have better anti-ghosting capabilities.
Does the keyboard tester work on mobile devices? +
While our keyboard tester is primarily designed for physical keyboards connected to computers, it can also be used with external keyboards connected to mobile devices. The on-screen virtual keyboard of mobile devices cannot be tested with this tool.
Is my keyboard data stored or transmitted? +
No, our keyboard tester runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any information about the keys you press. Your privacy is completely protected.
